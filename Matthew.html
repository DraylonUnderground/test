Five Recommendations 1) The YouTube video needs embedding in the instructions to avoid the user having to navigate away from the site. 2) For some of the instructions I would send the details to another page e.g. ‘For more information about Master Seed, click here’ 3) There are a few clunky grammatical issues. 4) The language used needs a little more standardisation. For example, some of the pages are referred to as ‘pages’, and sometimes as ‘screens’ 5) Overall I would break up the Getting Started page into smaller pages.
